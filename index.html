ABOUT US

Digital technologies are now the most fundamental drivers of change of this century, reshaping economies, government and civil society, and the ways in which they interact. “e-resilience.kg” is an initiative of the Computer Science Faculty of the University of Central Asia that aims to help Kyrgyz public and private organizations, policymakers, businesses, academia, and media to utilize ICT for societal resilience and economic development. Through training programs, research, public discussions, and other events of international standards we will help Kyrgyzstan get resilient in the Digital World.
