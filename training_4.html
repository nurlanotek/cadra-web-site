Regional Universities Network

Regional Universities Network aims to be a starting point for establishing Central Asian Digital Resilience Academy. The groundwork for future expansion will be initiated at the regional level by working with selected universities in the region to contribute intellectually to the plans and potential curriculum of the proposed Central Asian Digital Resilience Academy (CADRA). Partner universities: 

  • Tajikistan : Khorog State University
  • Kyrgyz Republic: Naryn State University, American University in Central Asia, Polytechnic University
  • Kazakhstan: Kazakh-British Technical University
  • Uzbekistan: Tashkent University of Information Technologies
  • Afghanistan: Badakhshan University
  • Pakistan: Karakoram International University
As part of the network, the representative universities will decide the curriculum and teaching methodologies that would be most appropriate for a Central Asian Digital Resilience Academy (CADRA).
