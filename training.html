Course Outline

Data Privacy

  • Data privacy concepts
  • Policy for Data Privacy
  • Data privacy by-design
  • Data portability and cross-border transfer of data
  • Responsibility for data protection
  • Discussion or homework on data privacy
  • Case studies + Review questions

Cybersecurity

  • Cyber security Fundamentals
  • What is Cyberspace?
  • What is Cyber security?
  • Why is Cyber security Important?
  • Modern cybersecurity
  • Risk assessment
  • Cybercrimes / Cyber Security Breaches (phishing, identity theft, cyberstalking, harassment)
  • Types of cyber-attacks (password attacks, etc.)
  • Cybersecurity in clouds
  • Internet security / types of malwares (trojan, viruses, worms, spyware)
  • Cybersecurity problems
  • Mobile security (no Credit Card Numbers, place Lock on Phone, don’t Save Passwords, no Personalized Contacts Listed)
  • Social network security (don’t reveal location, keep birthday hidden, don’t link accounts, etc.)
  • Prevention software (firewalls, anti-virus etc.)
  • Case study + Review questions

Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery

  • Business continuity plan (BCP) and Disaster recovery planning (DRP)
  • Business impact analysis (BIA)
  • Threats and risks analysis
  • Impact scenarios
  • Preparedness
  • Implementation and testing

4.Regional Universities Network

Regional Universities Network aims to be a starting point for establishing Central Asian Digital Resilience Academy. The groundwork for future expansion will be initiated at the regional level by working with selected universities in the region to contribute intellectually to the plans and potential curriculum of the proposed Central Asian Digital Resilience Academy (CADRA). Partner universities: 

  • Tajikistan : Khorog State University
  • Kyrgyz Republic: Naryn State University, American University in Central Asia, Polytechnic University
  • Kazakhstan: Kazakh-British Technical University
  • Uzbekistan: Tashkent University of Information Technologies
  • Afghanistan: Badakhshan University
  • Pakistan: Karakoram International University
As part of the network, the representative universities will decide the curriculum and teaching methodologies that would be most appropriate for a Central Asian Digital Resilience Academy (CADRA).
