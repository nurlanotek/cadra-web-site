Trainings for Civil Society Organizations

  • Eligibility: Civil society representatives from registered organizations (NGO, Academia, Media etc.). 
  • The course is 100% online, beginner level and is instructed in Russian.
  • What will you learn: Data privacy concepts, overview of cyber security and cloud connectivity, internet security basics, and business continuity and disaster recovery.
  • Upon successful completion of the course, you will be awarded a certificate. 
To register, please e-mail us at digital.resilience@ucentralasia.org or click here (link to the Office Forms).
